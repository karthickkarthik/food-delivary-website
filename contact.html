Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track your order from your profile page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, Credit/Debit Cards, UPI payments, and various digital wallets.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's prepared. Go to order tracking page and click on "Need Help" to cancel.

What is your refund policy?

If you're not satisfied with your order, we offer full refunds. Contact support within 24 hours of delivery.

Do you have minimum order requirements?

Minimum order value varies by restaurant. Check the restaurant page for specific requirements.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time varies by location and restaurant, typically 30-60 minutes. Check estimated time at checkout.
